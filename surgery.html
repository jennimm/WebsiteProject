Computer-Assisted Surgery

Computers are now not only being used to teach and plan major surgeries but also aid in carrying out the actual surgery. Computer sofware is used in labs and medical schools to teach medical students abour surgery in many different ways such as augmented reality surgery, where the student is put in to a virtual surgery scenario or just to provide baisc information and training about surgery. A major breakthrough in medical technonlogy is robotically-assisted surgery (RAS) which allows for robots and software to complete a surgery in the least invasive way possible. The surgery is still under control by a surgeon using the robots and software.
